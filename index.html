Source & methodology. NYPD Complaint Data (Historic + Current YTD), NYC Open Data. All felony and misdemeanor assault complaints with valid coordinates, 2006–2026 YTD. Locations are aggregated to a ~110m grid for the source data and then summed into 300m hexagonal bins for display. Precinct station artifact. NYPD geocoders frequently fall back to a precinct station's address when an incident's actual location is unknown. About 26 such fallback locations account for roughly 1.7% of all complaints; these are excluded by default to avoid false hotspots at police stations. Toggle "Exclude precinct stations" off to include them.
